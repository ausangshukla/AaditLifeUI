LIFESTYLE COACHING PROGRAM

Aadit Life offers a structured personal coaching relationship that helps you set and achieve wellness goals with a focus on sustainable lifestyle changes. Typically, the coaching lasts six months until these changes are ingrained as habits. The program starts with a baseline evaluation from which a personalised plan is made. Every six to eight weeks, the evaluations are repeated to monitor progress and update the plans. While the focus of every program varies based on what's appropriate for the individual, a typical engagement covers cardiovascular fitness, gait analysis and running form, functional strength, flexibility, nutrition, and topics around stress and its physiological impact.
